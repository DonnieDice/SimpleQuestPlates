[SIZE=6][COLOR="#58be81"]What is SQP?[/COLOR][/SIZE]

[COLOR="#58be81"][B]SQP | Simple Quest Plates![/B][/COLOR] enhances your World of Warcraft experience by displaying quest progress icons directly on enemy nameplates. Know at a glance which enemies you need to defeat for your quests, how many items they drop, and your overall progress - all without cluttering your interface.

As part of the [COLOR="#8B1538"][B]RGX Mods[/B][/COLOR] collection, SQP is actively maintained and supported by our vibrant [URL="https://discord.gg/N7kdKAHVVF"][COLOR="#7289DA"][B]Discord community[/B][/COLOR][/URL]. Join us for instant support, feature requests, and to connect with thousands of WoW players!

[SIZE=6][COLOR="#58be81"]Features[/COLOR][/SIZE]

[LIST]
[*][B]Quest Icons[/B] - Display quest progress directly on enemy nameplates
[*][B]Fully Customizable[/B] - Adjust colors, fonts, sizes, and positions
[*][B]Item Priority[/B] - Shows item counts before kill counts when both are needed
[*][B]Multi-Language[/B] - Supports English, German, Spanish, French, and Russian
[*][B]Lightweight[/B] - Minimal performance impact with efficient caching
[*][B]Combat Options[/B] - Hide icons during combat or in instances
[*][B]Live Preview[/B] - See changes in real-time in the options panel
[*][B]Smart Detection[/B] - Automatically tracks all active quests in your area
[*][B]Position Control[/B] - Place icons on left or right side with X/Y offsets
[*][B]Font Customization[/B] - Size, outline style, and shadow effects
[*][B]Color Options[/B] - Different colors for kill, item, and progress quests
[*][B]Icon Tinting[/B] - Optional icon color tinting
[*][B]Account-Wide[/B] - Settings saved across all characters
[*][B]Error-Free[/B] - Comprehensive error handling
[*][B]Slash Commands[/B] - Quick access to all features
[/LIST]

[SIZE=6][COLOR="#58be81"]Command Reference[/COLOR][/SIZE]

[CODE]
/sqp                    - Open the options panel
/sqp help               - Display all available commands
/sqp test               - Test quest detection on current nameplates
/sqp on                 - Enable the addon
/sqp off                - Disable the addon
/sqp status             - Show current settings
/sqp scale <0.5-2.0>    - Set icon scale
/sqp offset <x> <y>     - Set icon position offset
/sqp anchor <left/right> - Set which side of nameplate to anchor to
/sqp reset              - Reset all settings to defaults
[/CODE]

[SIZE=6][COLOR="#58be81"]How It Works[/COLOR][/SIZE]

[SIZE=5][COLOR="#4ecdc4"]Quest Detection[/COLOR][/SIZE]
SQP uses Blizzard's quest API to detect which enemies are related to your active quests:
[LIST]
[*]Automatically scans all nameplates in view
[*]Updates in real-time as you accept/complete quests
[*]Prioritizes item drops over kill counts for dual-objective quests
[/LIST]

[SIZE=5][COLOR="#4ecdc4"]Performance[/COLOR][/SIZE]
[LIST]
[*]Efficient caching system for quest data
[*]Minimal CPU usage with smart update cycles
[*]No impact on frame rates
[/LIST]
